About Me

I am a Master of Science student in Structural Engineering at the University of Alberta, where I am pursuing advanced research in sustainable construction and structural engineering. My academic journey began at the University of Tehran, where I earned my Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering with a GPA of 16.54/20 (approximately 3.5/4).

My research interests center on sustainable timber construction, green building design, permanent wood foundations, and structural health monitoring. I am passionate about developing innovative solutions that combine structural engineering excellence with environmental sustainability.

Currently, I work as a Research Assistant at GRAF (University of Alberta) under the supervision of Dr. Hossein Daneshvar, supporting research in civil and structural engineering with a focus on sustainable and environmental applications.
